Challenge

As an agency, McGarrah Jesse wanted to celebrate International Women's month 2021 in a meaningful way.

Solution

We created a month-long campaign recognizing the women who have helped us along our journies to where we are now. While recognizing that this work often goes uncelebrated. First we kicked off International Women’s Day by hand-writing letters to women we admire. Then we invited women everywhere to join the project all month long for International Women’s Month.

We collected the letters and displayed them proudly on a dedicated website to pay homage to those who came before us and to make a promise to share our strength with those who come next.
